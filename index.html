How Our Technology Works

Our intelligent infrared system follows a simple three-step process to automate your home environment

1. Detection

Advanced infrared sensors detect when someone enters the room, capturing presence and body temperature data

2. Analysis

Our intelligent system analyzes time of day, occupancy patterns, and environmental conditions in real-time

3. Action

Automated control activates lights and fans based on conditions: both at night, fans only during day

Advanced Features

Smart Presence Detection

Advanced infrared sensors detect when someone enters or leaves a room, automatically triggering appropriate responses.

During nighttime, both lights and fans activate. During daylight hours, only fans are controlled for optimal energy efficiency.

Temperature-Based Fan Control
